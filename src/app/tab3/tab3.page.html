About AMY Capture Now

It's a camera application which click the picture, allow turning the camera front and back and let the user upload picture from the gallery.



Plugins Used

  • Camera

    To capture the image

  • FileSystem

    To save the clicked picture to the device.

    To load image to the applcation from the device
