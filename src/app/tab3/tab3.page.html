About AMY Capture Now

AMY Capture Now

It's a 2 in 1 app that includes a torch and a camera.

The torch enables the flashlight to see in the dark

The camera captures the image and save it to the device. The saved file is then loaded back to gallery from the device.

Here, we are using the following plugins

  • Camera

    • To capture the image

  • FileSystem

    • To save to device and then load the image back from device

  • Flashlight

    • To turn on the flashlight
